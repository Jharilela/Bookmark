Amazing Features

Since day 1, we had our users in mind while developing the app. We wanted to make it easy and convinient for people to share books with others.

Large collection of books

Search through a vast library of books located in Google, Amazon and Goodreads database.

Track your books

See who has borrowed or bought your book. Never loose sight of your favorite books again

Buy or borrow books

Whenever you want to read a physical book, just find someone near you that has the book, chat them up and request to borrow the book

5km Radius

Borrow or buy books from people near you within a proximity range of 5km. Get notified when someone in your proximity has the book you want to read

Sell/Rent books

Don't leave your books to collect dust on the shelf, let others read them too

Excellent Support

Our primary focus is to make your sure your books are in the right hands

1. Find a book

that you want to read

Browse through a large section of books and see whichever one interests you. We use Google, Amazon and Goodread's book libraries to give you the best results.

Don't know what to read?
No problem, we list out the books that are available near you. Now you can read a new book every day with minimum effort

2. Find people near you

who has the book you are looking for

Our book community consists of book lovers in many parts of the world. Each user lists the book they own on their shelves for other poeple to see. They can add a price for renting the book and another price to sell the book. You can now find people who have the book within a proximity range of 5km near you

  • Buy or borrow books
  • Find books the person has
  • Find what books they want to read
  • Set a meetup time
  • Suggest a meetup location

3. Meet people

and connect with them

You can personally message people and set up a meeting date and location. Here you can also discuss the terms of the trade, discuss the renting price, buying price, book exchange, etc.

The maps feature within the chat gives the distance between you and the other person along with the nearest locations. It becomes very convenient to meet people and requires minimal time
